Our Core Values

The First Core Values are the cornerstones of our LEGO robotics program. They are among the fundamental elements that distinguish FIRST LEGO League from other programs of its kind.

By embracing the Core Values, participants learn that friendly competition and mutual gain are not separate goals, and that helping one another is the foundation of teamwork.

We express the FIRST philosophies of Gracious Professionalism® and Coopertition® through our teaching and spirit.
